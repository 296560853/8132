EVENT

past activities

Autopilot Sharing and Live Demo

Guest speaker Jonathan Tse and Dennis Wu, founders of HK Society of Autonomous Model Vehicle, shared with participants how to train the Autopilot Donkey Car, with Tensorflow. A real track was built to demonstrate how to collect data and build AI model for the autopilot car.

AI Workshop 101

AI workshop was held for students to learn the top AI news, the must-know AI terms. It included a hand-on workshop for students to build their first Image classification model to distinguish cat and dog with simple python code using Google Colab.

AI Movie Sharing

10+ AI movies were used as an example to explain the most popular AI applications. It’s not a traditional "one-way" sharing class, audience were also invited to discuss whether these AI movie scenes have already come to our real life! The class aimed to explain AI concept and it’s current application in a casual way.

Industry Sharing on Data Science

AI sharing by Hien Luu from LinkedIn and Ganesh from Databrick who flew all the way from San Francisco. They explained how AI was being applied in LinkedIn, and introduced industry trends and career path in data science and engineering.

AI Company Visit

Students visited two AI companies in Hong Kong Science Park, Sensetime and Master Dynamic. Participants learnt basic machine learning concept and gained insights of the AI industry.

AI Career Talk

An AI career sharing was conducted for CUHK undergraduates. During which, the latest trend of AI industry and possible interview questions were presented and discussed.
